# 🏔️ Frankivsk Space

**Frankivsk Space** is a city-focused IT organization created to support the digital growth of Ivano-Frankivsk, local businesses, students, developers, and community projects.

Our goal is to build a practical IT portal for the city — a place where local businesses, organizations, and communities can get modern digital services such as websites, CRM systems, booking tools, admin panels, automation, and custom software.

We believe every modern city should have its own IT space: a local digital ecosystem where businesses can find technical help, students can gain real project experience, and developers can build useful products for the city.

## 🏙️ What we build

Frankivsk Space focuses on practical digital products for the city and region:

* Business websites and landing pages
* CRM systems and admin panels
* Booking and reservation platforms
* Local service directories and city portals
* Internal business automation tools
* Educational and internship projects
* Open-source modules and reusable solutions
* Digital products for local communities and organizations

## 🚀 Mission

Our mission is to help Ivano-Frankivsk grow as a modern digital city by connecting technology, business, education, and community.

We want to make IT more accessible for local entrepreneurs, students, and organizations by turning real business needs into real working software.

## 🌱 Education & internships

Frankivsk Space is also designed as a practical learning environment for students and junior developers.

Instead of only learning theory, contributors can work on real projects, improve production code, review architecture, build features, fix bugs, and understand how modern web products are created.

We support learning through:

* Real project practice
* Mentorship and code reviews
* Open-source contribution
* Team collaboration
* Frontend, backend, and DevOps experience
* Business-oriented product thinking
